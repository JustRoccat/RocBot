About me

JustRoccat is an emerging artist who stepped into the music scene with his debut track on 12/06/23. Despite being relatively unknown at the moment, JustRoccat has been quietly honing his craft and developing a unique musical identity. His early work reflects a passion for experimentation, blending various musical styles and incorporating Phonk production methods, often weaving in atmospheric textures. As he continues to evolve as an artist, JustRoccat is poised to make his mark in the industry, offering listeners a fresh and distinctive perspective on music.
